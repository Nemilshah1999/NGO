Go Green Organization is a non-profit organization that initiates, develops and manages reforestation and environmental conservation projects across India. We work with an ambitious goal to plant and care for trees and help people reduce their carbon foot print while simultaneously generating livelihood options for rural farmers.

Our Mission & Our Vision

 

Go Green Organization is on the mission to create a cleaner, greener and better planet. To achieve this, we believe in planting trees in those areas that are devoid of them. Along with our greening efforts, we strive to uplift and assist the rural communities.

We at Go Green Organization aspire to bring people, trees and the environment closer through technology.We provide our planters an online platform to plant and track their trees while supporting socio- environmental causes. We endeavour to provide convenience, transparency and clarity through our technology platforms. 		  Our vision is to develop a lush-green and a bio-diverse, sustainable world for the future generations. We intend to create a world where everyone is enthusiastic about planting trees, which would create a repository for a rich ecological bio diverse planet.

Through planting of trees we aspire to bring economic sustainability to poor rural communities and empower them with livelihood opportunities.We are working towards creating a just and sustainable society .To take a step ahead, we invite more corporates and individuals to partner with us in initiatives that make a tangible difference to the environment, rural livelihoods and the education of children.
